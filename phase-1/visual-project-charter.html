About this presentation

Format and origin

The Visual Project Charter was originally produced as a Google Slides deck, intended to give the project group a clear, accessible overview of the Project Charter in a presentation format. It is displayed here as a series of slide images, preserving the original visual layout and design.

Phase One context

This slide deck was produced during the kickoff phase of the AIUK Community Platform project. Its purpose was to introduce the project group to the scope and structure of the community platform work in a format suitable for a presentation or group review session.

The Visual Project Charter sits alongside the written Project Charter document as part of the Phase One outputs. Together, these artefacts set the foundations for the work — establishing a shared understanding of what the project was aiming to achieve, who was involved, and how the work would be organised across the subsequent phases.

Producing a visual version of the charter helped ensure that all members of the project group — regardless of their familiarity with formal project management documents — could engage with the project scope and goals from the outset.
