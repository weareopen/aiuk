About this presentation

The Recognition Ecosystem deck was produced during Phase Four as part of the Community Strategy Design work. It brings together the outputs of co-creation workshops and stakeholder input to define a coherent approach to recognising and rewarding participation in the AIUK community.

The framework covers four interconnected areas: badges awarded for specific contributions and achievements; trust levels that reflect a member's standing and unlock new capabilities; community roles such as moderators, mentors, and champions; and the underlying principles that guide how recognition is structured to be fair, transparent, and motivating.

Phase Four context

Phase Four — Community Strategy Design — translated the research findings from Phases Two and Three into concrete design proposals for the AIUK platform. The recognition ecosystem was one of several interconnected strategy outputs from this phase, alongside persona development, user journey mapping, and a KPI framework.

The recognition work draws on established community platform practice and was adapted to the specific needs and values of the AIUK membership — balancing incentives with authenticity, and individual acknowledgement with collective contribution.
